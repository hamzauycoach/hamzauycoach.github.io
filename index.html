Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial? +

Yes! We offer a 3-day free trial for all new members. You can explore our facilities and try out classes before committing to a membership.

Can I freeze my membership? +

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 2 months per year for medical or travel reasons. Just contact our front desk.

What should I bring on my first day? +

Bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide lockers and shower facilities.

Are personal trainers included? +

It depends on your plan. Premium members get a consultation, while Elite members get 4 sessions per month. Additional sessions can be purchased separately.
